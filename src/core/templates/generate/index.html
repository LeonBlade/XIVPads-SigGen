Create a new template

A template is the blue print for a signature, you can place down a background and then decide what information should be shown, where to show it, how to show it. You can personalize your design with extra assets, fonts, colors. Once you are happy with your template you can use it right away, share it with friends or publish it to the site for all to use! No restrictions!
